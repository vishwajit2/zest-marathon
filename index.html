Rules and Regulations

  • Participants have to use strava app to record the distance and time of racing.
  • The event will be conducted through third party app. Participants will be provided with a BIB Number for unique identification send via your respective emails.
  • Since the race is Flex Time type, it can be attempted at any time during the above specified “Schedule of events”. However, the race attempted at times apart from the time window of the event will not be considered for evaluation.
  • After recording the reading, participants have to take screenshot of it.
  • Participant have to submit the screenshot and activity link of strava profile to google form link which will be provided to you by organiser.(Compulsory)
  • Before submitting the screenshot in google drive link rename your screenshot to bib number allotted to you by organiser.(Compulsory)
  • Without changing the screenshot name to bib number, screenshot will not consider for evaluation.(Compulsory)
  • Participants can also record their small video while running or selfie and upload it later on google drive link for after movie of this event.(Optional)
  • FUN-RACE(3-KM) winners will only get goodies and E-certificate.
  • For other category cash prizes will be there.
  • All participant will get E-certificate for participation in marathon and winner will receive E-certificate for winning marathon.
  • If we find any discrepancy in data that you entered ZEST has authority to readily debar you from the race.
  • Make sure you don't violate any government rules related to lockdown . i.e. you need to follow all the rules of local authority regarding the lockdown and you need to maintain social distancing during marathon.
  • There will be no flag off or inauguration take place before marathon.
  • Avoid public gatherings and running in crowded areas.
